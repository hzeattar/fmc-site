Brand & logo use

Use of the FMC name and crest is restricted. Media may reproduce our logo solely to illustrate genuine reporting about the Commission. It must never be used in a way that implies the FMC endorses a firm, product, or service. Firms claiming "FMC approval" using our crest should be reported to press@fmc.org.uk.

Information for journalists (FAQ)

Will the FMC comment on a specific firm or live investigation?

We do not confirm, deny, or comment on individual firms or ongoing investigations. Concluded enforcement actions are published in full as final notices, which we are happy to discuss.

How do I verify a claim that a firm is FMC-regulated?

Search the firm on the public register. If you need help interpreting an entry or a notice, the press office can assist on background.

Can I receive releases automatically?

Yes. Email the press office to join the media distribution list for embargoed and on-release statements, and to receive data under embargo where appropriate.

Do you provide interviews and broadcast facilities?

Subject to availability, we can arrange interviews with spokespeople and have facilities for broadcast at our London headquarters.
